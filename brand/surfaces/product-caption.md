# Product Caption Surface

Product captions explain Sora’s system logic in short, accurate language. They are used wherever the viewer needs a quick explanation of what the material is doing.

## Purpose

Use this file for:

- Product diagrams
- Slide captions
- Social captions
- Website explainer copy
- Mode labels
- UI callouts
- Governance descriptions
- Final project appendix notes

## Caption Role

Captions should make the product understandable without overclaiming.

A good Sora caption answers:

- What is happening?
- What triggers it?
- Which mode is active?
- Why does it matter?

## Core Product Description

Sora is a responsive roofing material that shifts between Reflect Mode and Absorb Mode based on roof surface temperature.

## Short Caption Options

### General

- Responsive roofing for changing conditions.
- A roofing material that adapts to roof surface temperature.
- Designed to help homes respond to increasingly extreme climates.
- One roof system with two temperature-responsive modes.

### Reflect Mode

- Reflect Mode lightens the roof surface when temperatures rise.
- A lighter roof surface helps reflect heat from the roof.
- Activated when roof surface temperature moves above the set threshold.
- Designed for hot roof conditions and direct solar exposure.

### Absorb Mode

- Absorb Mode darkens the roof surface when temperatures drop.
- A darker roof surface helps absorb warmth at the roof.
- Activated when roof surface temperature moves below the set threshold.
- Designed for cooler roof conditions and changing seasonal exposure.

### Roof Zones

- Each roof zone can respond independently based on its own surface temperature.
- Different roof planes may shift at different times depending on sun exposure and temperature.
- Zone behavior helps the roof respond to real conditions across complex roof geometry.

### Control Interface

- The control interface shows mode status, surface temperature, and roof zone behavior.
- Manual override allows the homeowner to temporarily hold a selected mode.
- Sync All applies one selected mode across all roof zones.

## Technical Accuracy Rules

Always say:

- Roof surface temperature
- Reflect Mode
- Absorb Mode
- Responsive roofing material
- Manual override
- Independent roof zones

Do not say:

- Weather-responsive if it implies forecasts drive the system
- Smart roof
- AI-powered roof
- Mood-responsive
- Solar-powered unless solar integration is specifically shown
- Energy-saving unless the claim is qualified or substantiated
- Self-healing, self-aware, living, or sentient

## Caption Lengths

### Micro Caption

Use for labels and small callouts.

**Reflect Mode**  
Lightens when roof surface temperature rises.

### Short Caption

Use for slides, social posts, and website cards.

**Reflect Mode** lightens the roof surface when temperatures rise, helping reflect heat from the roof.

### Medium Caption

Use for product explainers or final project notes.

Sora shifts between Reflect Mode and Absorb Mode based on roof surface temperature. Each roof zone can respond independently, allowing the material system to adapt to changing exposure across the home.

## Final Project Use

Use captions throughout the final brand book to show editorial authority. Captions should explain why a choice was made, not simply describe what is visible.

Weak:

> This is a house with a Sora roof.

Stronger:

> This image tests whether the Reflect / Absorb mode system can remain clear in a realistic residential environment without changing the home’s architecture or roof pattern.

## Do

- Be specific and accurate.
- Keep product behavior tied to roof surface temperature.
- Explain human direction when discussing process.
- Use captions to clarify what the image is proving.
- Keep language calm and concise.

## Do Not

- Overpromise measurable performance.
- Use generic marketing filler.
- Describe Sora as a full smart-home system.
- Add scientific claims that are not in the project.
- Let captions become too long for the surface.

## Quality Check

A good product caption should make the system clearer in one sentence. If it creates new questions or implies an unsupported feature, revise it.
